BMGT407 - INFORMATION SYSTEMS PROJECTS
BMGT407 is the capstone course for Information Systems (IS) majors. The class aims to help develop real-world IS solutions for business clients. Through a complete IS development process, students design and implement functional prototypes to prepare them for careers in technology and consulting.
